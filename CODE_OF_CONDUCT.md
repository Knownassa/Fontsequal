# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We commit to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and considerate.
- Respect different backgrounds, identities, and experience levels.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Protect private information and security reports.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, doxxing, or repeated disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to project maintainers. Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community.
